## Methodology Notes

1. **Yuma Framework:** Subnet tokens replace internal operational expenses. Fundamental value = (Annual OpEx / 365) / Daily Miner Emissions.
2. **Comparable OpEx:** For subnets without public revenue, we estimate what the equivalent centralized service would cost (OpenAI API, AWS compute, etc.)
3. **Emissions safety margin:** 10% buffer added to daily emissions assumption.
4. **Miner sell pressure:** Assumes miners must cover hardware/power costs, so external buyers must step in to sustain price.
5. **Speculative subnets:** Price < 0.003 TAO + low fill = likely worthless. Price > 0.05 TAO with no use case = bubble risk.
